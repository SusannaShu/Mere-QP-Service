QP BLAZE: Community Meal Mini-Game

In the heart of the market, colors collide, QP Blaze dances, with flames as its guide. It celebrates flavors from lands far and near, A symbol of unity, bringing us cheer. As cultures converge, igniting the night, In Long Island City, we find our shared light.

Celebrate Connection Through Food

QP Blaze invites you to explore the rich diversity of flavors and cultures that define our community. Food is more than sustenance—it’s a bridge that connects us across borders, traditions, and histories. This interactive game is a celebration of that unity, where every dish tells a story and every choice builds a shared meal.

Community Meal Mini-Game

Match dishes to their cultural origins to build a community meal!
